How it works

The Import Process

Importing a pet into Nigeria requires precise timing and correct documentation. Our team at Waggies manages the entire timeline to ensure no delays at Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport.

warning
Plan Ahead Start process 1-2 months before travel to avoid quarantine issues.

Consultation & Microchipping

We assess your pet's breed, size, and health status. We ensure an ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip is implanted before any vaccinations.

description

Permits & Documentation

We procure the Nigerian Import Permit from NAQS. We also guide your local vet on issuing the correct Health Certificate and Rabies Titer Test results.

flight

Flight Booking & Travel

Booking the most direct route to Abuja. We verify the IATA crate setup and ensure the airline manifest is correct for live animal transport.

home_pin

Customs Clearance & Delivery

Upon arrival, our agents handle customs and quarantine clearance instantly, then deliver your pet safely to your doorstep in a climate-controlled vehicle.

Compliance

Required Documentation

Failure to provide correct documentation can result in quarantine delays or repatriation. We review every document before departure to ensure a seamless entry.

  • check
    Valid Vaccination Record Rabies vax > 30 days & < 1 year. DHLPP for dogs (Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza).
  • check
    Import Permit Issued by Nigerian Agricultural Quarantine Service (NAQS). We procure this on your behalf.
  • check
    Veterinary Health Certificate Issued by official government vet in origin country within 7 days of travel.
  • check
    Microchip Certificate ISO 15-digit microchip number must match all documents perfectly.
