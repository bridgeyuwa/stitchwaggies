Common Questions

How early should I start the process? expand_more
For most international destinations, we recommend starting the process at least 3-4 months in advance. Countries with strict rabies quarantine laws (like the UK or South Africa) may require up to 6-7 months of preparation.
Can my pet fly in the cabin with me? expand_more
This depends on the airline and the size of your pet. Generally, only small pets (under 8kg with carrier) are allowed in the cabin on specific airlines. Most international pet relocations from Nigeria occur via Manifest Cargo for safety and regulatory reasons.
Is it safe for my pet to travel in cargo? expand_more
Yes. The cargo hold for live animals is pressurized, temperature-controlled, and dimly lit to encourage sleep, just like the passenger cabin. We only book with pet-friendly airlines that have established safety protocols.
