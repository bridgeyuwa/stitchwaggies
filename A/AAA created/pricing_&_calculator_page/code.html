Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require a deposit for booking? expand_more
Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure dates for boarding and training services. This deposit is fully refundable if cancellations are made at least 48 hours prior to the check-in date.
Are there extra charges for medication administration? expand_more
Standard oral medications are administered at no extra cost. For injections or complex medical care, a small daily fee may apply. Please discuss your pet's specific needs with our team during booking.
How does relocation pricing work? expand_more
Relocation costs are highly variable depending on the destination (Domestic vs. International), airline fees, crate requirements, and veterinary certifications. Our base consultation fee covers the initial planning and quote generation.
