03. User Responsibilities

To ensure the safety of all pets and staff, clients must adhere to the following responsibilities:

Vaccinations:

All pets must have up-to-date vaccinations. Proof of vaccination (e.g., Rabies, DHPP) is required prior to boarding or grooming.

Behavioral Disclosures:

Owners must disclose any history of aggression or anxiety. Waggies reserves the right to refuse service to pets that pose a danger to staff or other animals.

Health Status:

Pets with contagious illnesses should not be brought to the facility without prior veterinary clearance.

All prices are quoted in Nigerian Naira (NGN). Payment terms are as follows:

  • Late Fees: A late fee of 5% per week may be applied to overdue invoices for long-term boarding clients.
