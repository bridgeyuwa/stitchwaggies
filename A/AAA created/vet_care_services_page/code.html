Featured Service

Microchipping: Permanent ID for Your Pet

A microchip is the only permanent method of identifying your pet. It lasts a lifetime and greatly increases the chance of being reunited if your pet gets lost.

filter_1

Quick Implantation

A simple procedure similar to a vaccination. The chip, size of a grain of rice, is placed under the loose skin between the shoulder blades.

filter_2

Global Registration

We handle the registration of your pet's unique ID number into a global database, linked to your contact details.

filter_3

Lifetime Protection

Unlike collars that can break or fall off, a microchip is permanent. Most vet clinics and shelters scan for chips immediately.
