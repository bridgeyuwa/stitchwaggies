Frequently Asked Questions

Do my points expire? expand_more
Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned. As long as you book at least one service a year, your account remains active!
Can I use points for Vet services? expand_more
Yes! You earn points on Vet consultations and can redeem them for discounts on future checkups. However, points cannot be used for prescription medications.
How do I redeem my points? expand_more
Simply let our staff know at checkout, or select "Redeem Points" when booking online through your dashboard.
