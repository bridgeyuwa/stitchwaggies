General Queries

Where is Waggies Pet Services located? expand_more
We are conveniently located in the heart of Abuja. For security and privacy of our boarded guests, the exact address is provided upon booking confirmation. However, we offer pick-up and drop-off services across all Abuja districts including Maitama, Asokoro, Wuse II, and Gwarinpa.
What are your operating hours? expand_more
Our facility is staffed 24/7 for our boarding guests. For drop-offs, pick-ups, and grooming appointments, our reception is open Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sundays from 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
Is there a vet on-site at all times? expand_more
Yes, we have a resident veterinary technician on-site 24/7 to monitor all pets. Our senior veterinarian is present during business hours and is on-call for any after-hours emergencies.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept bank transfers, POS payments (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), and cash. For international clients booking relocation services, we can also accept international wire transfers or USD payments upon request.

Boarding & Daycare

What vaccinations are required for boarding? expand_more
To ensure the safety of all our guests, we strictly require up-to-date vaccination records for Rabies, DHLPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza), and Kennel Cough. Records must be submitted at least 48 hours prior to check-in.
Can I bring my dog's own food? expand_more
Absolutely! In fact, we highly encourage it to prevent stomach upset. Please bring enough food for the duration of the stay, labeled with your pet's name and feeding instructions. If you prefer, we can provide our premium house diet for an additional daily fee.
How much playtime do the dogs get? expand_more
Standard boarding includes 3 supervised play sessions per day in our secure, grassy outdoor yards. For high-energy breeds, you can upgrade to our "Active Paws" package which includes additional one-on-one fetch sessions or agility training.
Do you accept aggressive dogs? expand_more
For the safety of our staff and other guests, we conduct a temperament assessment for all new dogs. We generally do not accept aggressive dogs for group play, but we may offer "Solo Boarding" where the dog is handled individually by senior staff, subject to assessment.
Can I visit my pet during their stay? expand_more
While we understand you miss them, visits can often cause separation anxiety and stress for the pet when you leave again. We recommend video calls instead! We offer daily photo/video updates via WhatsApp so you can see how much fun they are having.

Pet Relocation (Import/Export)

Do you handle international pet export from Nigeria? expand_more
Yes, we are experts in pet travel. We handle the entire export process from Abuja or Lagos to virtually any country, including the UK, USA, Canada, and EU. Services include flight booking, export permits, veterinary health certificates, and IATA-approved crates.
How long does the import permit process take? expand_more
For importing pets into Nigeria, the import permit typically takes 3-5 business days to process once we have all required documentation from you. We recommend starting the process at least 2 weeks before your travel date.
Do you provide IATA compliant crates? expand_more
Yes, we stock a full range of IATA-compliant travel crates. If you are relocating with us, we will measure your pet to ensure they have the exact size required by airlines for safety and comfort. We also offer custom wooden crates for giant breeds.
What is the blood titer test requirement? expand_more
Many countries (like the UK, EU, and South Africa) require a Rabies Neutralizing Antibody Titer Test (RNATT). This test usually requires a 3-month waiting period after the blood draw before travel. We facilitate the blood draw and shipment to approved labs abroad.

Grooming & Spa

Do you sedate pets for grooming? expand_more
We operate with a strict "humanity over vanity" policy. We generally do NOT sedate pets for grooming. However, for extremely aggressive or matted dogs where grooming is medically necessary, sedation can be performed ONLY by our on-site veterinarian with your written consent and monitoring.
How often should I groom my dog? expand_more
This depends on the breed. Long-haired breeds (like Lhasa Apsos, Poodles) typically need grooming every 4-6 weeks to prevent matting. Short-haired breeds can benefit from a wash and de-shedding treatment every 8-12 weeks.
What does a "Full Groom" include? expand_more
Our Full Groom package includes a pre-brush, warm water bath with premium shampoo, blow dry, full body haircut/styling, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and anal gland expression (if needed).

Vet Care

Do you offer microchipping? expand_more
Yes, we offer ISO-compliant 15-digit microchips which are required for international travel and are the gold standard for pet identification. The procedure is quick, similar to a vaccination shot, and provides permanent ID for your pet.
Can you handle emergency surgeries? expand_more
Our clinic is equipped for routine soft tissue surgeries (spay/neuter, mass removal). For complex orthopedic or specialized emergency surgeries, we stabilize the patient and refer to our partner specialist hospital in Abuja while managing the transport.

Pricing & Booking

Do I need to pay a deposit? expand_more
Yes, for boarding and relocation services, a 50% deposit is required to secure your dates. The balance is due upon check-in (for boarding) or prior to flight booking (for relocation).
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancellations made more than 7 days before the service date are eligible for a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 3-7 days forfeit 50% of the deposit. Cancellations less than 48 hours prior are non-refundable.
Do you offer discounts for long stays? expand_more
Yes, we offer a 10% discount on boarding fees for stays longer than 30 days. We also have loyalty packages for frequent grooming clients.
