Introduction

At Waggies Pet Services ("Waggies", "we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors to our web site and our clients in Abuja and internationally. In this Privacy Policy, we provide detailed information on when and why we collect your personal information, how we use it, the limited conditions under which we may disclose it to others, and how we keep it secure.

This policy applies to all services offered by Waggies Pet Services, including pet boarding, grooming, veterinary care, microchipping, training, and our pet relocation services (import/export/local).

2

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

person Personal Identification

Name, email address, phone number, and physical residential address necessary for billing and pick-up/drop-off services.

pets Pet Information

Pet names, breed details, medical history, vaccination records, microchip numbers, and dietary requirements.

credit_card Transaction Data

Details about payments to and from you and other details of services you have purchased from us.

flight_takeoff Relocation Data

Passport copies, flight details, and destination addresses for import/export services.

3

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • check_circle
    Service Delivery To process bookings, provide veterinary care, and manage boarding schedules.
  • check_circle
    Communication To send you updates about your pet's stay, appointment reminders, and quote responses.
  • check_circle
    Legal Compliance To comply with local Nigerian laws and international travel regulations for pet relocation.
4

Security Measures

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered, or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know. They will only process your personal data on our instructions and they are subject to a duty of confidentiality.

5

International Transfers (GDPR)

For our clients utilizing our pet relocation services (Import/Export), it may be necessary to transfer your personal data and your pet's data to countries outside of Nigeria.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure that your personal data is protected by requiring all our international partners to follow the same rules when processing your personal data. We rely on Adequacy Decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission for such transfers.

6

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site. We use Google Analytics to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. We do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone.

7

Your Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

Request access to your personal data.

Request correction of your personal data.

Request erasure of your personal data.

Object to processing of your personal data.

Request restriction of processing your personal data.

Request transfer of your personal data.
