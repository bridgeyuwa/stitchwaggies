Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a grooming session take? expand_more
A typical full grooming session takes between 2 to 4 hours, depending on the size of your pet, breed, and coat condition. We prioritize your pet's comfort and will never rush the process to ensure a positive experience.
Do you sedate pets for grooming? expand_more
No, Waggies maintains a strict no-sedation policy. We use patience, positive reinforcement, and gentle handling techniques. If a pet is too aggressive or stressed, we will stop the service for their safety and discuss options with you.
Can I stay with my dog during the grooming? expand_more
For safety reasons, we recommend owners do not stay in the grooming area as dogs often get excited or anxious seeing their owners, which can make working with sharp tools dangerous. You are welcome to wait in our comfortable reception area.
